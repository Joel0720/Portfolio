Project Calculator

Purpose:

During my years at secondary school I quickly developed a deep fascination with mathematics. What intigued me the most was the problem solving aspect of maths but we often as stundents needed some assistance when faced with a lengthy or complex calculation. So calculators were the best option "in my opinion" for making those calculations more easier to deal with.

Functionality:

So this J.Calculator was modelled after a casio calculator I would have used during secondary school, all the way up to university. J.Calculator includes basic functions such as addition, subtraction, multiplication and division. I also wanted to add some more advanced functions such as square root, square and cube to make sure it did indeed resemble a casio calculator. Feel free to test it out on your right!!
